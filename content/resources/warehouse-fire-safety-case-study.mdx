## The site

A multi-bay warehouse and distribution facility in Gujarat, running two
shifts with mixed racking, loading-bay and office areas — a common layout
where extinguisher coverage and signage tend to lag behind the building's
actual footprint as storage areas expand.

## The gaps found

A [fire risk assessment](/services/fire-risk-assessment) identified
under-coverage in the newer racking bays, faded and non-compliant exit
signage, and no recent staff training record on file.

## The response

Extinguisher coverage was brought up to IS 2190 spacing requirements across
all bays, exit and equipment signage was replaced with photoluminescent
signage meeting ISO 7010 pictograms, and a full staff training session was
run covering extinguisher handling and evacuation procedure.
