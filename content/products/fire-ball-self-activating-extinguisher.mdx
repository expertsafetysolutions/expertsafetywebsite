A lightweight, hands-free alternative to a conventional extinguisher for situations where trained operation isn't guaranteed — roll or throw the ball toward the base of a fire and it activates automatically within 3–10 seconds of contact with flame, releasing its dry chemical charge without anyone needing to pull a pin or aim a hose. Effective across Class A, B, C, E and F fires, non-toxic and environmentally friendly, making it suitable for homes, vehicles, kitchens and workshops as a first-response unit anyone can use.
