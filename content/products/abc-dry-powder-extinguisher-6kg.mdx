The 6 kg ABC dry powder extinguisher is the standard first-response unit for most industrial and commercial premises. Its mono-ammonium phosphate charge smothers solid, liquid and gas fires, making it the most versatile single unit you can mount on a factory floor, in a warehouse aisle or beside an electrical panel. This Kanex-manufactured, BIS-approved and ISI-marked unit is hydraulically tested with anti-corrosive treatment, finished in UV-resistant pure polyester powder coating, and fitted with a brass chrome-plated squeeze valve and an EPDM braided hose that stays flexible instead of cracking with age. The Unique Gauge Testing System (UGTS) lets our service technicians verify pressure without discharging the cylinder. Supplied fully charged, pressure-tested and tagged, with mounting bracket and operating instructions — refilling and annual testing are available under our maintenance contracts.
