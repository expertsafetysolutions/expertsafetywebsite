A low-cost, single-shift mask for general housekeeping, sweeping, loading and other light-dust tasks where a reusable cartridge respirator would be overkill. The moulded non-woven shell holds shape through a shift, and a foam nose seal cuts fogging and gapping. Supplied in bulk packs so sites can keep every visitor and contractor covered without tracking reusable stock.
