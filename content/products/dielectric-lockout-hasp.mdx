Where metal hardware near live circuits is a hazard in itself, this hasp is moulded from flexible, lightweight, non-conductive polymer instead of steel — safe for electrical lockout applications where a metal hasp could conduct current or spark. Six holes allow group lockout, and dual-jaw scissor designs (25mm and 38mm) cover different padlock shackle sizes in one unit.
