AFFF foam extinguishers blanket burning liquids and seal vapours, preventing reignition — the reason they are specified around fuel storage, paint stores, generator rooms and chemical handling areas. This Kanex 9 litre stored-pressure unit is effective on Class A and Class B fires, BIS approved and ISI marked, with a brass chrome-plated squeeze valve, air-aspirated nozzle for a higher fire rating, and EPDM braided hose. Not for use on electrical fires.
