A trolley-mounted, CO₂-cartridge-driven foam or water extinguisher for large-area Class A/B fire risk that a portable unit can't cover — fuel storage yards, warehouse aisles, plywood and paper stacking areas. The brass forged cap has an auto safety release mechanism, and the PVC braided hose carries a stainless steel protection spring over its full length to resist kinking during rapid deployment. Not for use on electrical fires.
