Photoluminescent signage charges under normal lighting and stays visible for hours after power loss — a requirement for escape routes in most industrial fire NOC conditions. Printed on rigid non-toxic substrate with a high-luminance pigment, available in left, right, up and down arrow variants plus running-man pictograms.
