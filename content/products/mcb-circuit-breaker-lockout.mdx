Clips directly over an individual MCB toggle switch, blocking it from being flipped back on until the padlock threaded through it is removed. Available in pin-in and pin-out styles to suit different breaker toggle designs, and in single units, sets of 2, or combo packs covering multiple breaker types on one distribution board.
