Urea-based (Monnex-type) potassium bicarbonate powder knocks down flammable-liquid and electrical fires faster than standard ABC powder, which is why it is specified for high-risk applications such as fuel handling, generator rooms and chemical process areas. The cylinder is epoxy-polyester coated internally and pure polyester coated externally for UV resistance, anti-corrosion treated through a seven-tank hot-dipping phosphate process, and helium leak-tested to the same 1×10⁻⁸ mbar·l/sec standard as the rest of the Kanex range.
