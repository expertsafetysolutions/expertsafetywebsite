A clear, special-grade transparent cover that locks over a panel-mounted switch, rotary handle or push-button, blocking operation while still letting technicians see the switch's on/off position through the cover. Round and square versions are available in small and large sizes, with cut-outs to accommodate handles that rotate 45° or 90°.
