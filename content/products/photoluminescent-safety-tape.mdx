Self-adhesive photoluminescent tape run along staircase edges, corridor floors and handrails to mark the escape route with directional arrows or chevrons, glowing when other lighting fails — the low-level equivalent of an exit sign for guiding people at floor height through smoke. Also available in black-yellow and red-yellow hazard stripe for non-photoluminescent hazard marking.
