Built for sites running a structured lockout programme rather than ad-hoc padlocks — a 6-level keyed management system supporting differ-key (one worker, one key), alike-key and master-key configurations (2 keys per lock plus 1 master key per 10 locks). The glass-fibre reinforced nylon body is waterproof and anti-corrosion treated, with an anti-skid grip that works with gloves on, and a key-retaining shackle that can't be left unlocked and walked away from.
