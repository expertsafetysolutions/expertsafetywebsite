The stored-pressure counterpart to the cartridge-type mobile foam extinguisher — pressurised with nitrogen at the works rather than by a field cartridge, and fitted with a spray nozzle to lift the effective fire rating. A 300 mm stainless steel protection spring on the PVC braided hose keeps it flexible under repeated deployment. Suited to loading bays, tank farms and production halls needing large-volume Class A/B cover on wheels. Not for use on electrical fires.
