A weighted traffic cone with a reflective collar band for marking work zones, wet floors and vehicle exclusion areas around a site. The 710mm PU version is built for durable, high-frequency road and site use; the 650mm PVC version is a lighter option for indoor and low-traffic marking. Both flex and return to shape after vehicle contact rather than cracking.
