Where appearance matters as much as protection — receptions, boardrooms, food and pharma production floors — this Kanex stored-pressure unit swaps the usual powder-coated shell for a smooth stainless steel body. It carries UL-listed MAP powder rated for Class A, B, C and electrical fires, a CE-marked brass chrome-plated valve and an EPDM braided hose that stays flexible instead of cracking with age. Every unit is helium leak-tested down to 1×10⁻⁸ mbar·l/sec and hydraulically tested before it leaves the works, and the Unique Gauge Testing System lets your maintenance team confirm pressure without discharging the cylinder.
