A stored-pressure alternative to the external-gas-cylinder mobile extinguisher — this unit is pressurised with UHP-grade nitrogen at the works rather than relying on a field-fitted CO₂ cylinder, simplifying deployment when every second counts. The brass forged valve has an auto safety release mechanism, and the PVC braided hose carries a 300 mm stainless steel protection spring to prevent kinking during rapid unwinding. Suited to loading bays, tank farms and large production halls.
