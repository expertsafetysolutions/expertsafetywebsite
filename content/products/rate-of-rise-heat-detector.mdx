Heat detectors suit environments where smoke detection would false-alarm constantly — kitchens, boiler rooms, dusty production areas and loading bays. This unit combines a fixed upper-temperature threshold with rate-of-rise sensing, so it triggers both on a sudden temperature climb and on an absolute limit.
