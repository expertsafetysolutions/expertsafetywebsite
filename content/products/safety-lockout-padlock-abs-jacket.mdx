The core tool of any lockout programme — one padlock per worker, keyed so only that worker can remove it. The ABS jacket comes in six colours for department or crew identification, each set of three ships with 3 keys and a drafted instruction sticker. Available with long or regular shackle, and with photo-ID or engraved name/department labels for larger sites running formal LOTO programmes.
