FE-36 clean agent gas leaves zero residue and zero ozone depletion potential, so it protects switchgear, data storage, laboratory instruments and other equipment that dry powder or foam would damage or contaminate. Unlike CO₂, FE-36 discharges as a gas rather than a liquid and does not leave condensed moisture behind. The unit is UL listed and US EPA SNAP approved, with the same brass chrome-plated squeeze valve, Unique Gauge Testing System and stainless pull pin used across the Kanex range.
