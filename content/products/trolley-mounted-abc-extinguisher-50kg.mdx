Where a portable unit cannot deliver enough agent, a 50 kg trolley-mounted extinguisher provides sustained discharge over a much wider area. This Kanex unit is mounted on a steel trolley for rapid deployment across yards, loading bays, tank farms and large production halls, propelled by an external CO₂ gas cylinder through a PVC braided hose with a 300 mm stainless steel protection spring to prevent kinking. The brass forged cap has an auto safety release mechanism, BIS approved and ISI marked, hydraulically tested to standard.
