Built to UL File No. EX28681, this stored-pressure unit is specified where a facility's fire safety programme follows US-style compliance rather than BIS alone — common on multinational plants and warehousing operated to a global standard. The steel cylinder is MIG-welded by 6G certified welders with a protective skirt, finished in advance automatic powder coating, and backed by a 12-year hydrostatic test interval and six-year warranty. Stainless steel handles, lever and pull pin resist corrosion in coastal and high-humidity sites.
