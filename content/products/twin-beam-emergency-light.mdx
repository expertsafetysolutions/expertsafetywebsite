Twin LED beam heads on a rounded-dome housing switch on automatically the moment mains power fails, running on battery until the supply resumes — then trickle-charges back to full readiness. Non-maintained (off until power fails) and maintained (always-on, longer backup) versions are available, and a combined EXIT-sign-plus-twin-beam version doubles as directional signage during the outage it's lighting.
