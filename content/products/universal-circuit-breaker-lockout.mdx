A single device that adapts to single, double or triple-pole circuit breakers via a folding screw clamp, rather than needing a different lockout for every breaker type on the panel. A self-foldable-screw version removes the need for a screwdriver entirely, speeding up lockout during shutdowns. A padlock through the body completes the isolation.
