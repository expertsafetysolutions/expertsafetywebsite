A stainless steel-bodied stored-pressure unit available with an AR-AFFF foam charge for Class A and B fire risk or a plain water charge for Class A alone. The polished finish suits kitchens, laboratories and public-facing installations where a painted steel cylinder would look out of place, while the underlying construction — CE-marked brass valve, EPDM braided hose, helium leak testing — matches the rest of the Kanex range. Not for use on electrical fires.
