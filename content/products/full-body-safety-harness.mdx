A full-body harness distributes fall-arrest forces across the thighs, pelvis and shoulders rather than the waist alone, which is why it's the standard for any task above 1.8 m. This harness has a dorsal D-ring for lanyard connection, quick-release buckles for fast donning, and padded shoulder straps for extended wear during roof, tank and scaffold work.
