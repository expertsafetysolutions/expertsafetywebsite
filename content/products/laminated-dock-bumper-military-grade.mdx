Loading bay walls and dock edges take repeated hard contact from reversing trucks and trailers. This military-grade bumper is built up from layered tyre laminates bonded to a gun-metal backing plate, absorbing that impact rather than transmitting it to the structure behind — the difference between a scuffed bumper and a cracked dock wall.
