A flexible multi-stranded steel cable with clear plastic insulation threads through side-by-side breaker panels or gate valves and locks into an ABS body that resists chemical exposure. Accepts up to 4 padlocks for shared multi-technician lockout, with high-visibility, write-on safety labels included so every lock is traceable to the person who applied it.
