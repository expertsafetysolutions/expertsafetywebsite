Locks out three-phase MCCBs (227V and above) that a standard MCB lockout can't grip — moulded teeth bite onto the breaker lever for a strong hold, fitting levers up to 3 inches wide and 0.9 inches thick. Rugged stainless steel and PVC construction resists corrosion in plant environments, with normal-screw and foldable-screw fitting options.
