Locks over the handle of a ball valve so it can't be turned while a line is isolated for maintenance. The standard model covers valves from 9.5mm to 31.5mm (3/8" to 1¼"); long-open and double-leg variants extend coverage up to 2½" (63.5mm), with optional side extensions for awkward handle geometries.
