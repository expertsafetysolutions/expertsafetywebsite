Valves, gate wheels and equipment that don't have a matching lockout device can still be isolated by threading a galvanised, vinyl-coated steel cable through the mechanism and closing it off with this injection-moulded, corrosion-free lockout body. Rated for up to 8 padlocks and -20°C to +55°C service, with cable lengths from 2m up to 20m for larger multi-point isolations.
