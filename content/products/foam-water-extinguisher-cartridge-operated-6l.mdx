This cartridge-operated unit uses a CO₂ gas cartridge to propel either an AFFF foam charge (Class A and B) or plain water (Class A), letting maintenance teams recharge it on site rather than sending it out for pressure refilling. The rugged cartridge-type build with EPDM braided hose is a common fit for plywood and paper stores, petrol stations and hospitality kitchens where Class A and B liquid-fuel risk needs a dedicated, low-residue agent. Not for use on electrical fires.
