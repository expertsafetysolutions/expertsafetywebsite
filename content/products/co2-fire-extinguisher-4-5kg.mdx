Carbon dioxide extinguishers leave no residue, which makes them the correct choice for server rooms, switchgear, control panels and laboratory environments where powder would damage sensitive equipment. This Kanex 4.5 kg unit ships with a high-pressure CCOE/PESO-approved seamless cylinder conforming to IS:7285, a discharge horn on a bend pipe, and a wheel-operated controllable discharge valve to IS:3224 with a pressure relief disc. Also available with a squeeze-grip valve or in a lighter aluminium body for easier handling. Hydraulic testing and refilling are carried out in-house and certified.
