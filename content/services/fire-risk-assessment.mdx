Before you can fix a fire safety gap, you need to know exactly where it is.
Our risk assessment gives you a structured, documented view of hazards,
equipment coverage and compliance status across your entire site.

## The assessment process

A qualified assessor walks the full site — production floors, storage areas,
electrical rooms, loading bays — mapping fire loads, ignition sources and
existing protection against the applicable IS and NFPA reference codes.
Extinguisher coverage, hydrant reach, alarm zoning and escape route adequacy
are all checked against occupancy and floor area.

## What you receive

The output is a written report ranking findings by severity, with a
prioritised remediation plan and indicative costs for each item — so you can
sequence spend sensibly rather than fixing everything at once. The report is
formatted to support fire department NOC submissions and insurance audits,
and we offer a follow-up re-assessment once remediation work is complete.
