An Annual Maintenance Contract moves fire safety from a once-a-year scramble to
a managed programme. Instead of coordinating separate vendors for
extinguishers, hydrants and alarm panels, one contract and one point of
contact covers the entire system.

## Coverage

AMC visits are scheduled around the statutory interval for each appliance
type, with functional testing of alarm panels, detectors, call points and
sounders alongside extinguisher and hydrant servicing. Contract tiers range
from inspection-only to full parts-and-labour coverage — we'll recommend a
tier based on your risk assessment and site criticality.

## Priority response

Sites on an active AMC get priority scheduling for breakdown callouts, so a
faulty detector or a discharged extinguisher doesn't sit unresolved until the
next scheduled visit. We track every contract's renewal date and reach out
ahead of expiry so cover never lapses unnoticed.
